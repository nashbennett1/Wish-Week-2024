MVHS Wish Week

Hallways and Decorations

During Wish Week, the school hallways undergo a remarkable transformation as students and faculty come together to decorate them in support of the Make-A-Wish Foundation. The hallways are decorated with vibrant and creative displays. Students and teachers show their spirit and enthusiasm by participating in various activities such as holding bake sales and engaging in fundraising competitions between grades or homerooms. The entire school is filled with a sense of excitement and unity as everyone works towards a common goal of making a difference in the lives of children with critical illnesses.
