MVHS Wish Week

Wish Week Assembly

The Harry Potter themed Wish Week assembly was a magical event that brought the enchanting world of Hogwarts to life. Students and teachers alike immersed themselves in the wonder of the Harry Potter universe, with the halls adorned to resemble the iconic settings of the beloved series. The assembly featured various activities, such as dancing, a light show, and the choosing hat, reminiscent of the experiences at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.
