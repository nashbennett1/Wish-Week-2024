MVHS Wish Week

Fundraising

Wish Week is a popular fundraising event held by schools, typically lasting a week and consisting of daily activities. During Wish Week, students can create their own ways to raise funds or combine their efforts with existing school events like homecoming or spirit week. The event can include various activities such as opening assemblies, spirit days, sales events, sporting events or tournaments, and 'thons. Students are encouraged to set a fundraising goal, promote the event through online fundraising, and use posters, banners, and flyers to spread awareness.
